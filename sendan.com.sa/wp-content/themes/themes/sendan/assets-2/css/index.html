Message from CEO

Our website reflects the exciting journey of SENDAN Group since we started in 1994 in Jubail Industrial City, Saudi Arabia. SENDAN has expanded vertically & horizontally and has also established its presence in the western region of Saudi Arabia and United Arab Emirates. We have also diversified by including allied businesses involving trading of industrial materials and manufacturing of temperature sensors.

Through continuous development of our human capital, acquisition of latest technologies, strong adherence to quality and safety by adopting best industry practices, SENDAN continues to grow and enhance its reputation among its clients. We develop local skills/crafts meeting our business requirements and keep our pace in meeting or exceeding environmental standards by imparting training & development of resources and social responsibility. We, at SENDAN, continue relentlessly to stick to our commitments and hope we have the support of all our well-wishers so that we may thrive.

“Without continual growth and progress, such words as improvement, achievement, and success have no meaning.”
(Benjamin-Franklin)
